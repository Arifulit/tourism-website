This historical mosque holds great significance and is a testament to the architectural beauty of the Mughal era. As you step into Dhanbari Masjid, you will be mesmerized by the exquisite mosaic and floor marble stone, showcasing masterful craftsmanship. Originally rectangular in shape, the mosque underwent renovations over the years and now boasts a square appearance. It features three arched entrances to the east and another one to the north-south, adding to its grandeur. Inside the mosque, you will find a central mihrab niche, adorned with grooved arches and intricate floral designs.
