Nagarpur Jamindar Bari

Now that you’ve learned about the Pakulla Jame Masjid, let’s move on to another fascinating destination in Tangail. Get ready to explore the historical significance and architectural marvel of the Nagarpur Zamidar Bari. The Nagarpur Zamidar Bari, established by Zamindar Jadunath Chowdhury in the 19th century, holds a special place in the history of Tangail. Spread over approximately 54 acres of land, this magnificent estate showcases a blend of Western and Mughal architectural styles. As you step into the premises, you’ll be greeted by numerous small and big houses, adorned with beautiful white stone. The main building of the Chowdhury Bari stands tall, accompanied by notable structures like the nahbat khana, guest hall, and horse building. Each of these buildings features intricate artistic craftsmanship, with designs that will leave you in awe. The jhulan dalan, used for puja archana, and the pari dalan and rangmahal add to the grandeur of the estate.
