Jalchhatra Pineapple Market

When you visit Madhupur, don’t miss out on experiencing the bustling atmosphere and vibrant activity of the Jalchhatra Pineapple Market. This market is a hub of pineapple trade and offers a unique glimpse into the pineapple industry in Bangladesh. The pineapple cultivation methods in Madhupur are renowned for producing high-quality pineapples, particularly the Jaldugi and GiantQ varieties. Farmers from all over Madhupur bring their pineapples to the market, where buyers from different parts of the country come to purchase them at wholesale prices. The market is not only limited to pineapples, but also offers other fruits like bananas, kamranga, amla, and kachor. In recent years, there has been an upward trend in the pineapple market, with an increasing demand for Madhupur’s pineapples. This has opened up new opportunities for pineapple exports from Bangladesh. The market provides a platform for local farmers to showcase their produce and connect with potential buyers. The success of the Jalchhatra Pineapple Market has encouraged the growth of other pineapple markets in Garo Bazar, Madhupur Bazar, and the 25 Mile Market. Visiting the Jalchhatra Pineapple Market not only allows you to witness the vibrant trading activity, but also offers a chance to explore the picturesque pineapple gardens and immerse yourself in the aroma of ripe pineapples. So, make sure to add this lively market to your itinerary when you visit Madhupur.
